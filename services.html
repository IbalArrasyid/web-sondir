Our Services

As a Civil Engineering Specialist Consultant

Gamma Solusi Optima (GSO) was established in Bekasi on October 1, 2019. It embodies the aspirations of its founders to actively contribute to National Development in Indonesia by providing technical consulting services in collaboration with government and private entities. Despite our relatively young age, GSO boasts a team of reliable and experienced experts across various fields. We continually develop innovative and sustainable solutions to address the unique challenges faced by each client.

Initially focused on Civil Engineering Detailed Engineering Design and Surveys, GSO has diversified its services. Presently, we offer expertise in road and airport planning, mining infrastructure, oil and gas facilities, industrial facilities, and more. Since our inception in 2019, GSO has experienced rapid growth, successfully completing over 20 foundation-related projects. Some notable projects include Ahmad Yani International Airport, Karanglo Underpass, Balikpapan Bay Toll Road, Jakarta-Cikampek Toll Road, Cisumdawu Toll Road, Jetty Coal Buntok, Tawang Mosque PT. KAI, Damai Bridge, Plaza Statue Ir. Soekarno, PLTU Suralaya, West Java II Road and Bridge, Jambi-Rengat Toll Road, among others. We have also engaged in collaborative contracts to further expand our impact.
